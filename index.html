'Yoga Gently'

Easy and gentle classes, suitable for all ages and anyone looking for a gentle hour of nourishment for the body and soul. Yoga is for every body - shape size and ability. Gentle Asana (yoga postures) promote flexibility, a sense of wellbeing and joy. Being aware of the breath in Pranayama (breathing techniques) we develop our own sense of inner calm. Enjoy some time out of the now to be in the moment to restore and relax in the union of mind and body. Yoga is a place to retreat, renew and heal, no matter where you are or the environment to practice, go inside to find a sanctuary that's all your own.

The goal of yoga is not the head-stand, shoulder-stand or power flow - there are many classes out there providing those Asana in terms of pace and strength, but a certain level of core stability and strength must be yours first, to safely enjoy the tough stuff - Yoga is also progressive – but not in the sense of injury to get there - by going back to the softer, gentler and spiritual side of Yoga, find the true essence and message - the breath

Each class is planned with safety and alignment in mind. GGentle warm ups for myofascial release help skeletal muscle immobility and pain by relaxing contracted muscles with breath awareness and an array of props. Chairs, straps, soft yoga balls, blocks, bricks, blankets and gentle movement. The blood, lymphatic and Autonomic Nervous Systems receive equal attention for energy renewal, rest and relaxation. By gently moving forwards backwards and rotating the spine from lumbar, thoracic and cervical verterbrae, supporting spinal muscles gain strength in order to create core stability. The abdominal muscles and organs are also gently moved, massaged and strengthened too, these are not strenuous movements but gently held, repeating and counter-posing movements. Supine, kneeling and standing Asana can all be adapted to support each body however needed. The wall and chairs provide great support for alignment and stability.
