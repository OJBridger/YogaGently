'Yoga Gently'

A very easy and gentle class, suitable for all ages and anyone looking for a gentle hour of nourishment for the body and soul. Yoga is for every body - shape size and ability. Gentle Asana (yoga postures) promote flexibility, a sense of wellbeing and joy. Being aware of the breath in Pranayama (breathing techniques) we develop our own sense of peace within. Enjoy some time out of the now to be in the moment to restore and relax in the union of mind and body. Yoga is a place to retreat, renew and heal, no matter where you are or the environment to practice, go insidto find a sanctuary that's all your own.

The goal of Yoga is not the headstand - Yoga connects body, mind and breath to build strength - move the spine gently - allow muscles and their connective tissues to soften and release. and perhaps most importantly, learning about how the breath can bring calm and serenity. Yoga brings a sense of well-being and balance in our life. Yoga Gently is just that, there are no power flows, no headstands.

Each class is planned with safety and alignment in mind. Gentle warm ups through the joints, spine, and muscles take us to floor Asana (Yoga postures) to move and flex the spine safely. Standing Asana are all very easy to follow. The wall and chairs provide great support for alignment and stability. Pranyama (breathing techniques) slow our breath and mind to draw us calmly into a warm, supported relaxation - sometimes a guided meditation is spoken, a song as a prayer is played - then finish (no pressure to do - listening is just as beneficial).

To share all the magic and joy Yoga brings, to see a student leave class with a glowing complexion, a lighter sense of ease and a smile - that’s what really being able to teach Yoga is about....for me anyway.
